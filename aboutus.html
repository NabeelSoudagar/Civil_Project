Infinity Group – You Dream, We Build

Infinity Group is not just a name, but a vision that aims to bridge the gap between engineering education and real-world application. We provide a unique and comprehensive platform where students are encouraged to develop not only their technical knowledge but also the right attitude, problem-solving ability, and practical skills that are essential to succeed in today's competitive world.

Our philosophy is simple yet powerful:

◆◆ Learning should not be limited to classrooms; it should be applied to create solutions, opportunities, and impact.

Infinity Group believes that every student has the potential to become an innovator, leader, and change-maker when given the right guidance and exposure.

Infinity Group's Legacy & Goals

"For 10 years, Infinity Group has been a trusted name in the Construction Industry, renowned for delivering projects of exceptional quality, innovation, and sustainability. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to bridge the gap into the realms of education, inspiration, and social entrepreneurship—creating a foundation not just for structures, but for dreams, aspirations, and a better future."

Our mission is to:

  • Provide hands-on learning experiences to engineering students.
  • Strengthen both technical knowledge and practical application skills.
  • Encourage a problem-solving mindset and the ability to tackle real-world challenges.
  • Create a collaborative ecosystem of industry experts, mentors, and peers.
  • Inspire students to think beyond employment and explore entrepreneurship.

Why Infinity Group?

  • For engineering students, we're more than a firm or an institution. We're a platform for transformation. Here, you will:
  • Gain practical, hands-on field experience.
  • Develop practical skills alongside academic learning.
  • Work on real projects and solve real-world problems.
  • Get opportunities to connect with industry experts, leaders, and mentors.
  • Build the confidence to turn your ideas into impactful solutions.

The Visionary Behind Infinity Group – Mr. Sampath S. Patil

Infinity Group was founded by Mr. Sampath S. Patil, a Civil Engineer, Entrepreneur, Innovator, and Social Worker from Belgaum, Karnataka.

Academic Background

Mr. Sampath completed his B.E. in Civil Engineering from Gogte Institute of Engineering, Belgaum, followed by an M.Tech in Structural Engineering from Gogte Institute of Technology, Belgaum. His strong academic foundation combined with a passion for social development laid the groundwork for what Infinity Group is today.

Awards & Recognitions

  • Mr. Sampath's work has been recognized at both national and international platforms.
  • "Best Leader" award at Indian Student Parliament in Pune and "Best Model Village" award for his work in social development, including his contribution to the Adarsh Gram Yojana at Modage Village.
  • Recognized as the "Best Leader" by Deshonnati, Infosys and Mr. Shankar Bidari (Former Commissioner, Police) at Hubli.
  • Felicitated by "Kannada Sahitya Parishath" for his contributions to social welfare and successful rural development projects in Belgaum.
  • Recognized at the "Visvesvaraya Technical University Youth Festival" in Bangalore for innovative ideas, with special appreciation from Dr. Ganesh Devdas (Former S. Min., Jaiprakash Narayan (Founder), Deshpande Foundation).

Contribution & Mission

His life has been dedicated to a mission with two faces:
1. Social Impact - Solving Rural problems, uplifting communities, and creating sustainable ecosystems.
2. Student Development - Guiding students to improve their skills, find the right job opportunities, and explore entrepreneurial possibilities.

His ultimate goal is to transform students from job seekers into job providers—nurturing a generation of engineers who innovate, lead, and contribute to society.

Our Promise

At Infinity Group, we can't build futures—we challenge you to design your futures. We don't just deliver projects—we deliver opportunities. We don't just train engineers—we nurture the next generation of innovators and change-makers.

◆ Infinity Group is where engineering meets innovation, where learning meets application, and where dreams turn into reality.
