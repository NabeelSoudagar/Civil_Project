Appreciation & Association with Eminent Personalities

Mr. Santosh’s initiatives have received admiration and support from distinguished national figures:

  • Mr. Sanjay Ghodawat — Visionary entrepreneur & philanthropist, Founder of the Sanjay Ghodawat Group, a diversified enterprise spanning education, FMCG, renewable energy, aviation, and real estate.
  • Mr. K. Annamalai — Former IPS Officer & Political Leader, served as Deputy Commissioner of Police, known for his discipline, leadership, and public service commitment.
  • Honorary Captain Yogendra Singh Yadav, PVC — India’s youngest Param Vir Chakra awardee, celebrated for extraordinary bravery and sacrifice during the Kargil War.
  • Captain Naveen Nagappa (Sena Medal) — Kargil War Hero, honored for courage and devotion to duty, now inspiring youth through motivational leadership and service to the nation.

A Legacy of Impact
