POST-CONSTRUCTION SUPPORT

Our commitment continues long after the final handover.

We believe in forging lasting relationships with our clients. Our post-construction services are designed to maximize the lifespan, efficiency, and performance of your new facility, ensuring sustained value for your investment.

OUR VALUE-ADDED SERVICES!

Warranty Management

Dedicated support to manage all supplier and contractor warranties, ensuring swift resolution of any issues post-occupancy.

O&M Training

Comprehensive training for your facility staff on the proper operation and maintenance of all new systems and equipment.

Facility Documentation

Delivery of complete, organized, and searchable documentation including as-builts, submittals, and maintenance schedules.

Preventative Maintenance

Customized service packages to schedule and execute preventative maintenance, minimizing downtime and costs.

Energy Optimization

Post-occupancy tuning and audits to optimize building systems for maximum energy efficiency and reduced utility expenses.

Space Reconfiguration

Support for future minor renovations, tenant improvements, and necessary adjustments to internal spaces.

Need Maintenance or Support?

Our quick-response team is ready to assist with any post-construction needs or service requests.
